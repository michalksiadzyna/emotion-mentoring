Mission

The Shen Foundation exists to take the Emotion Mentoring method further than private practice ever could. We advance the physical, mental, and spiritual development of individuals through structured mentoring, education, and direct support — giving more people access to work that creates real, lasting transformation.

Michał Ksiądzyna

Founder · Emotion Mentor · Researcher

Michał Ksiądzyna is the creator of the Emotion Mentoring method — a precise, structured approach to working with the human emotional system that produces lasting transformation in how people think, decide, and relate to themselves and others.

With a background in economics and strategy from the Wrocław University of Economics and Business, and years of deep study in the mechanics of emotional adaptation, Michał bridges analytical rigour with the subtlety required for genuine inner work. His published research and professional experience span business strategy, project management, and the psychology of performance.

He has guided executives, athletes, entrepreneurs, and high achievers through deep, structural emotional change — working across industries and continents. He founded the Shen Foundation to extend this work beyond private practice and make it accessible to those who need it most.
